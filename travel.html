Seeing the world before 30

My attempt to visit all 7 continents before I'm "old"

Asia

I lived in Asia for three years when I was young. Living in Hong Kong gave me a taste for travel and adventure! I went back to Asia to vist a friend in 2013, but I hope to make it back to Hong Kong some day to see how much it has changed.

In Asia I have visited the following countries:

  • China
  • Macau
  • Thailand (3 times!)
  • Indonesia
  • Malaysia
  • Singapore

Africa

I visited Africa in 2011 while my boyfriend was teaching English there. He was in Tanzania, living in a town called Moshi at the base of Mt. Kilimanjaro. We went on safaris in Tanzania, took an 8 hour bus across the country to visit the island paradise of Zanzibar, and spent time with Masaai tribe members.

North America

Born in Colorado, raised in Minnesota, schooled in Wisconson, and adulting in Chicago. Yet to travel Canada, but Banff is on my list. I've visited Mexico three times for fun in the sun. I love visiting new areas of the U.S., such as my recent trip to Charleston.
